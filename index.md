**System Signal** is for those who:

## 🧠 HOW I THINK

I believe product is more than execution. It's epistemology, systems design, and narrative.

> “All technical debt starts as storytelling debt.”

I think in layers:
- Systems are made of incentives  
- Incentives are shaped by models  
- Models are constrained by clarity  
- Clarity is your job as a product leader

I work on:
- Zero-trust data flows  
- Distributed decision systems  
- Fractal models of organization design  
- Making AI explainable and human-aligned

## 🧩 WHAT I BELIEVE

Ten operating principles I’ve tested through work:

1. **Product is the story the system tells itself**
2. **Don’t optimize noise — amplify signal**
3. **Simplicity is resolution, not reduction**
4. **Security is a system posture, not a patch**
5. **Design is incentive encoding**
6. **A great PRD is a blueprint and a bet**
7. **Trust compounds like interest**
8. **What you don’t model, you can’t manage**
9. **Every system is someone’s story**
10. **Scale = strategy × structure**
